Your miles on the ground gives you miles in the sky

  1. You can get 1 mile for Al-Fursan for every 6 riyals net without tax spent in NAFT stations using Naft Pass Prepaid Card or through NAFT i-PASS application.

  2. You can also get 1 mile for Al Fursan for every 2 SR net without tax to be spent at NAFT Formula service centers if payment is made in cash, mada, visa or NAFT i-PASS application.
